SONA’RMATI

Our Mission

To preserve and evolve the traditional art of Dokra by enabling custom, contemporary expressions that connect artisans and Gen Z audiences through sustainable design, digital accessibility, and cultural storytelling.

Our Vision

A world where every piece of clay and metal tells a story — where age-old craftsmanship inspires modern creativity, and local artisans thrive in a global, conscious community.

Our Values

Crafted by tradition. Shaped by you.

At Sonarmati, you create your own story through customizable Dokra art, crafted by skilled artisans. We care for the people, process, and planet—ensuring ethical, lasting design. You connect with heritage in a fresh, interactive way. And together, we define a new tradition—where culture meets creativity.
